## What Is CallHippo?

CallHippo is a cloud-based VoIP phone system trusted by over 5,000 businesses globally. You can get a virtual phone number in 50+ countries, set up IVR menus, record calls, integrate with your CRM, and manage your whole team's calling from a web dashboard or mobile app — all without touching a physical phone line.

It's particularly popular with sales teams, customer support operations, and distributed remote teams that need to look local in multiple markets without running actual office phone hardware.

The pitch is simple: plug in, pick your numbers, and start calling in under three minutes. Whether that's actually true depends on your setup, but it's not wildly off.

### The Free Basic Plan — What's Actually Included

The Basic plan is genuinely free — as in, $0 forever, not a trial. You get one phone number per user, voicemail, click-to-dial, SMS/MMS support, and the WhatsApp Business API integration thrown in at no cost.

The catch? No calling minutes are included. You pay per minute for any calls you make. For someone who just wants a business number to receive the occasional call and reply to texts, it's not bad. For anyone with actual outbound calling volume, you'll hit a wall fast.

---

### Starter — $18/user/month (Annual)

This is the entry point for teams doing real calling. You get 1,000 minutes for US/Canada calls per month, 100 SMS messages, basic reporting analytics, and the free Omnichannel Inbox (which covers Gmail, SMS, WhatsApp, Telegram, Facebook Messenger, and Instagram in one place).

What you don't get: call recordings, AI analytics, custom greetings, or call blocking. Those are locked to higher tiers. If call recordings matter to you — and for any sales or support team, they really should — you'll probably find Starter limiting pretty quickly.

### Professional — $30/user/month (Annual) ⭐ Most Popular

This is the sweet spot for most teams. The jump from Starter unlocks:

- **Unlimited** US/Canada calling minutes (vs. 1,000 on Starter)
- Call recordings (stored lifetime, not just 1 month)
- AI reports and analytics — summarized, AI-powered performance insights
- 500 SMS messages/month
- AI voicemail transcription
- Role-based access control
- Custom greetings and call distribution (cascading)

That unlimited calling is the big one. If your team makes more than a few hundred calls per month, the math works out heavily in Professional's favor vs. paying per minute on Starter.

---

### Ultimate — $42/user/month (Annual)

Ultimate is for teams that need the full enterprise-lite experience without going to an actual custom enterprise contract. On top of everything in Professional, you get:

- Dedicated account manager
- Single Sign-On (SSO)
- Custom integrations
- CRM-based VIP routing (automatically routes calls to the right contact owner)
- Holiday routing
- 1,000 SMS/month
- Call reminders and custom ring time
- 24/7 phone support (lower plans only get chat/email)

If your business relies on CRM data to route calls intelligently, or you have compliance needs that require SSO, this is probably where you land.

### Pro Suite (for Call Centers)

If you're running a high-volume outbound call center, CallHippo has a separate track — the Pro Suite — with Bronze, Silver, and Platinum tiers. These include features like:

- Live call monitoring (listen, whisper, barge)
- Skill-based routing
- Power dialer and predictive dialer
- Call whisper and call barging
- Gamification leaderboards for agents
- AI Smart DID Routing

Bronze starts at around $16/user/month (annual), Silver at $25, and Platinum at $40. Enterprise call center pricing is custom.

There's also a **Parallel Dialer** add-on product if you need simultaneous outbound dialing — that starts at $35/user/month for the basic tier.

## The Add-Ons Worth Knowing About

A few add-ons that come up a lot:

- **CallHippo AI** — $10/user/month extra. Gives you AI call transcription, sentiment analysis, key topic tracking, call summaries, and a smart dashboard. Worth it for sales coaching or QA-heavy support teams.
- **CNAM (Caller ID Name)** — One-time $100 setup fee. Makes your business name show up on caller ID. US-only, carrier-dependent.
- **Call Scribe** — Call transcriptions of recorded calls as an add-on.
- **Spam Watch** — Monitors your numbers for spam flagging. Useful if you're doing outbound at any scale.

The AI add-on is genuinely useful if you're managing agent performance. The rest depend on your specific use case.

## What Real Users Say

Across review platforms like G2, Capterra, and GetApp, the pattern in user feedback is pretty consistent:

The positives: setup is fast, support is responsive, call quality is solid, and the CRM integrations (HubSpot, Zoho, Pipedrive, Freshdesk, Zendesk) work well for most teams. Users repeatedly call out the quick onboarding and value-for-money on lower tiers.

The complaints: pricing can get confusing when you start adding users or add-ons, the per-minute rates for international calls can add up, and some users have had friction around cancellations. The mobile app UI has also drawn some criticism for feeling dated.

One Capterra reviewer summed it up well: the calling quality and budget are great, even if the chatbot support sometimes needs a human nudge to handle complex issues.

## Who Should Actually Use CallHippo

**Great fit for:**
- Small to mid-sized teams that need US/Canada calling + virtual numbers in other countries
- Sales teams wanting CRM-integrated calling with power dialer features
- Companies going international who need local numbers without local offices
- Remote teams that want calls, SMS, and WhatsApp managed in one inbox

**Might want to look elsewhere if:**
- You need very heavy Philippines or other specific-country calling (rates can be high)
- You're a solo freelancer who doesn't need a business system at scale
- You want everything included upfront with zero add-ons
